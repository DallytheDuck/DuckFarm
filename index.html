Where we are

Our farm is on the north coast of Scotland, and through our farm runs a river and a pond. We have chosen this place since we wish to keep our ducks as natural as possible in a natural environment. The ducks can meet deer, bagders etc. but we make sure that they do not get harmed or eaten.
